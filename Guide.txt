OFFICER AND UNIT
================
LDR is used to develop Order and Drill units.
Also highly used in unit's ATK and DEF calculation.

WAR is used to strengthen city Defense.

INT is used to develop Tech.

POL is used to develop Farm and Trade.

CHR is used to Recurit and Establish units.
Also used to Employ officers.

Maximum value of unit's Strength is 10000.
Maximum value of unit's Morale is 100.

Deployed unit's ATK is calculated using mostly LDR and a little of WAR.

Deployed unit's DEF is calculated using mostly LDR and a little of INT.

Damage in battle influenced by unit's Morale, unit's ATK, and target's DEF.

CITY
====
Gold income is based on Trade.
Food harvest is based on Farm.

Speciality is activated when Tech reached maximum value.

Order is reduced when Farm, Trade, Tech, or Defense is improved.
When Order is getting too low Revolt might occur and reduce multiple city stats.
Maintain the Order to avoid Revolt.

Order is also reduced when city is under attack.

City is taken by attacker when attacker successfuly reduced Defense to 0.
